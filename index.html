Product

Shell Kit
MCUSH tiny controller, various electronics modules, companied with an easy-to-use workbench software, Shell Kit helps kids/students startup their Python tutorial.
After being able to control the hardware with Python, their creativity will be inspired.
Shell Lab
MCUSH-based DAQ and controller, industrial level design. Shell Lab helps you controlling the real-world with Python.
